Rachel Keeley is a Manchester-based voice artist with a talent for reliability, warmth and consistency. Whether narrating an audiobook, voicing a character or giving a voice to a commercial project, Rachel consistently delivers clarity, warmth, and a delivery that feels alive.

Rachel's work covers audiobook narration, animation, video games and corporate film. Her voice acting ranges from character-driven audio drama to high-energy gaming.

Rachel works from her professional home studio where she records at 32-bit float, ensuring a high dynamic range and ultra-low noise floor. Rachel is also experienced with audio mastering, handling audio file production in-house.

She works to a fast turnaround and believes in a collaborative approach throughout the production process.
